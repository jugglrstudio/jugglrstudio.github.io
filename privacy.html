How we use information

We don’t collect personal information. The app runs on your device and uses local assets to provide its features.

Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data.

Children’s privacy

Jouzu is designed for learners of all ages. The app does not require an account and does not collect personal information. We do not show ads or use analytics in the current version.

If you (or a child) contact us for support, please have a parent or guardian send the email. If we learn that a child has provided any personal information to us, we will delete it.

We aim to comply with applicable child-privacy laws (e.g., COPPA) and Google Play’s Families Policy. Contact: jouzu.studio@gmail.com.
